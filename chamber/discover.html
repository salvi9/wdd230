Demographics

According to the most recent ACS, the racial composition of Rowland Heights was:

  • Asian: 64.72%

  • Other race: 12.95%
  • Two or more races: 3.62%

  • Native American: 0.51%
  • Native Hawaiian or Pacific Islander: 0.46%
