Site Name

Rowland Height's Chamber of Commerce

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to form an organization that promotes businesses found within the City of Rowland Heights with the goal to promote just the business but to drive attention to the city as a whole.

Scenarios

What should be expected from becoming a member of the Rowland Heights Chamber of Commerce? What are the purpose of the different memberships and their price points? Can i rescind my contract?
